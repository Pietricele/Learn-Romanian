Romanian Verb Conjugation Guide

Romanian verbs are conjugated according to person, number, tense, and mood. There are three main conjugation groups:

  • Group 1: Verbs ending in -a (e.g., a lucra)
  • Group 2: Verbs ending in -ea (e.g., a vedea)
  • Group 3: Verbs ending in -e, -i, or -î (e.g., a vorbi, a iubi)

Example: Conjugation of "a fi" (to be) in Present Tense
